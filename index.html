🎯 Mission & Vision

📍 Mission — AI & ML

What We Do

The mission of the Computer Science Department is to prepare students for careers as software professionals, Government Sectors, Entrepreneurship and for advanced studies in Computer Science and lifelong learning.

🔭 Vision — AI & ML

Where We're Going

  • Make the student to learn about the latest developments in the constantly developing field of Computer Science and to make them a good citizen for the nation.
  • Make the students to be actively contributing individually and in teams, ethically applying expertise to solve problems, and building on their knowledge to grow in their careers.
